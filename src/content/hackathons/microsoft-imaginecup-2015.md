Fresh from my **2014 ImagineCup victory**, I took on a mentoring role in Microsoft ImagineCup 2015, specializing in cutting-edge mobile technologies including **Xamarin cross-platform development**, **NFC (Near Field Communication)**, and **Bluetooth Low Energy (BTLE)**.
