## Project Overview

### The Hospitality Challenge

AccorHotels identified key challenges in hotel restaurant experiences:

- **Solo Dining Hesitation**: Business travelers reluctant to dine alone in hotel restaurants
- **Cultural Exchange Barriers**: Missed opportunities for guest interaction and cultural sharing
- **Restaurant Utilization**: Underutilized restaurant capacity during off-peak hours
- **Guest Experience**: Limited social interaction opportunities for travelers

### ShareMyTable Solution

Our platform connected hotel guests for shared dining experiences:

- **Social Dining Platform**: Match guests with similar interests for shared meals
- **Cultural Exchange**: Facilitate conversations between international travelers
- **Restaurant Optimization**: Increase restaurant occupancy through shared bookings
- **Enhanced Experience**: Transform solo business dining into social opportunities

## Technical Implementation

### Mobile Application Development

Built using Xamarin for cross-platform functionality:

#### Core Features

- **Guest Profiles**: Detailed profiles with dining preferences and interests
- **Smart Matching**: Algorithm-based matching of compatible dining companions
- **Reservation Integration**: Seamless booking with hotel restaurant systems
- **Real-time Messaging**: In-app communication for coordination and planning

#### User Experience Design

- **Intuitive Interface**: Easy-to-use design for diverse international users
- **Trust and Safety**: Verification systems and safety features for guest comfort
- **Accessibility**: Multi-language support and accessibility considerations
- **Offline Functionality**: Core features available without internet connectivity

### Backend Infrastructure

Leveraged Azure cloud services for scalability:

#### Data Management

- **Guest Profiles**: Secure storage of user preferences and dining history
- **Matching Algorithm**: Real-time compatibility analysis and suggestions
- **Restaurant Integration**: Live availability and booking management
- **Analytics Platform**: Usage metrics and optimization insights

#### Integration Capabilities

- **Hotel Systems**: Connection with AccorHotels property management systems
- **Restaurant POS**: Integration with restaurant reservation and ordering systems
- **Loyalty Programs**: Connection with AccorHotels Le Club loyalty platform
- **Payment Processing**: Secure payment handling for shared dining experiences

## Innovation and Features

### Smart Matching Algorithm

Developed sophisticated matching based on:

- **Dietary Preferences**: Cuisine types, dietary restrictions, and food allergies
- **Cultural Interests**: Language preferences and cultural exchange desires
- **Professional Background**: Business networking opportunities during meals
- **Timing Preferences**: Meal times and dining duration compatibility

### Social Safety Features

Implemented comprehensive safety measures:

- **Identity Verification**: Integration with hotel guest registration for authenticity
- **Rating System**: Post-dining feedback and reputation management
- **Emergency Features**: Quick access to hotel security and assistance
- **Privacy Controls**: Granular control over profile visibility and information sharing

### Restaurant Integration

Created seamless restaurant experience:

- **Table Optimization**: Automatic table size adjustment for shared dining groups
- **Menu Coordination**: Shared menu viewing and pre-ordering capabilities
- **Bill Splitting**: Automated payment division and processing
- **Special Offers**: Exclusive deals for ShareMyTable users
